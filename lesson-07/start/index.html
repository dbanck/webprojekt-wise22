A classic is back.

A cup that we hold in our hands with a lot of pride. A timeless design with its beautiful expression of minimalism, craft and quality. Throwing us back to the moment when we have finalised every detail and opened the doors of our beloved shop in Hamburg.

We are very happy to present you the Cup V3. We have improved the firing process and changed the porcelain to make it more durable. With its finely sandpapered outer surface it brings warmth to your hands while it remains crisp clean on the inside. The edges are sharp and precise, yet soft and welcoming.

This reinforced, third version of our tōrnqvist cup shall have its place in your shelf for many years to come.
